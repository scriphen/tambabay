About Tampa Bay

Tempa Bay Tourism Marketing District (TBTMD) is a Tourism Improvement District serving all areas within the City of Tempa Bay. Lodging businesses with 70 rooms or more within the City are assessed a two percent fee on each room night. TBTMD uses these dollars to support marketing and promotional efforts of a variety of programs, services and special events that increase room night sales for assessed hotels. This consistent source of funding for tourism marketing allows tempa Bay to maintain its status as a competitive, first-tier visitor destination and is vital to the strength and success of the City’s tourism economy.

Why Tourism Matter

Tempa Bay’s thriving economy depends on tourism. Thousands of jobs for our residents, billions in economic activity for our businesses, and millions in hotel taxes to our City—tourism benefits everyone in the City of Tempa Bay. Through hotel tax (or TOT) revenue, Tempa Bay’s out-of-town visitors help fund the services that keep our City safe, clean and a great place to live too.
